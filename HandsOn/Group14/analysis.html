Analysis Licensing of the Data Source

The general conditions allow us to reuse the documents for commercial and non-commercial purpose. The publisher and owner of the rights is the Ayuntamiento of Madrid

The general conditions for reuse:

1. It's forbidden to denaturalize the meaning of the information.

2. The source of the documents must be cited.

3. The date of the last update of the documents object of the reuse must be mentioned.

4. You can not indicate or suggest that the "Ayuntamiento" of Madridmparticipates, sponsors or supports the reuse carried out with the information.

5. The original metadata must be preserved.

6. It's expressly forbidden to perfom task of re-identification of people from these data.

Define Resource Naming Strategy

Resource Naming Strategy

The url-pattern of the URI that We've choosen: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/

The following URI's, were selected to describe the entities:

  • Domain: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/
  • Individuals path: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/
  • Individuals pattern: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/<resource_type>/<resource_name>
  • Ontological terms path: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/ontology/Place#
  • Ontological terms pattern: http://www.wsdlfiupm.es/resources/group14/ontology/Place#<place_name>
