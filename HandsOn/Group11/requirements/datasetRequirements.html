The selected dataset is about events and activities in the city of Madrid (R1).
The information is represented in various formats (R6): .CSV (R2), GEO, .JSON, .RDF, .XML also there is available a DCAT in .RDF.
It is open license for commercial and non-commercial purposes (R3).
The information about the legal conditions is available at:
http://datos.madrid.es/portal/site/egob/menuitem.400a817358ce98c34e937436a8a409a0/?vgnextoid=b4c412b9ace9f310VgnVCM100000171f5a0aRCRD
There is an option to download a GEO file that contains the ubication of the events and activities (R4).
The dataset has its own documentation at (R5):
http://datos.madrid.es/FWProjects/egob/DatosAbiertos/Deportes/AgendaActividadesDeportivas/Estructura_conjunto_datos_eventos_actividades_v1.doc
