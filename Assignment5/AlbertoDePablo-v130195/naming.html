Naming strategy

Hash URIs for ontological terms , slash URIs for individuals.

Domain: http://example.linkeddata.es/

Ontological terms path: http://example.linkeddata.es/myVocabulary#

Ontological terms pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/myVocabulary#<term_name>

Individuals path: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/

Individuals pattern: http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/<resource_type>/<resource_name>
