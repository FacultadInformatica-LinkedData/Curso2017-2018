Resource naming strategy

http://example.linkeddata.es/

http://example.linkeddata.es/instances

Individuals pattern:

http://example.linkeddata.es/instances/{resource_type}/{resource_name}

http://example.linkeddata.es/my_vocabulary#

Ontological terms pattern:

http://example.linkeddata.es/my_vocabulary#{term}
